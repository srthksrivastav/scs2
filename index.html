OUR MISSION

Education is the apprentice of life. Our vision is to provide value based education with new innovations and ideas so that our pupils grow into aesthetically rich, intellectually aware and disciplined.

OUR VISION

We embark on the mission of creating individuals who are confident about their potential and are goal oriented, sensitive to their environment and above all, co-creators of their own destiny.

OUR FOUNDER

Scholars Convent School, Gurugram owe their existence to Mrs. Nootan Srivastav, a noted educationist, who aimed at creating and providing a global educational environment in her school.

MESSAGE

A child is full of wonders. For him there is a lot to explore and a lot more to discover. We as their guardians need to provide them conducive environment which nurtures their curiosity and creativity.
